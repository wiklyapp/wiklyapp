Wikly in action

Your daily schedule at a glance

Wikly is designed for those who seek a full-featured tool for managing their daily lives in a more efficient and effective manner. Organize your day/week into tasks so that you can focus on completing them one at a time.

Organize your weekly routine

Create and manage your team with family members, students, or even your colleagues. Schedule, assign, and keep track of each person's weekly tasks and errands with ease.

Plan your day in a timeline

Wikly offers numerous visual ways to view and manage your daily tasks for greater efficiency. It is an easy-to-use, versatile, effective, and user-friendly tool that everyone can use to maintain a daily schedule.

Focus with reminders and widgets

With Wikly's reminders and widgets, you can stay on top of your schedule at any time. Wikly will remind you before a task starts or ends, and all of your upcoming tasks will be displayed in beautiful widgets on your home screen.

Effortless create tasks in seconds

You can create a task in a few seconds, and all you need to do is choose the people involved and add an appropriate title. You can also include a time and a duration. You can even add sub-tasks, colors, tags, and a description.
