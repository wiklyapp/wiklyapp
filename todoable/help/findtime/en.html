Find Free Time

What is Find Free Time?

Find Free Time helps you identify available time slots in your schedule for new tasks and meetings. It analyzes your existing commitments and suggests the best times to schedule new activities.

Quick Tip: Use this feature when you need to schedule focused work time or find meeting slots that don't conflict with your existing schedule.

How to Use

  1. Access the Feature

    Click the "Find Free Time" button in the top navigation bar or use the keyboard shortcut âŒ˜/Ctrl + F.

  2. Set Your Parameters
    • Duration: How long you need (30min, 1hr, 2hrs, etc.)
    • Time Range: Which days to search
    • Working Hours: Your preferred working time
  3. Review Suggestions

    The system will show available time slots, sorted by best fit. Green slots indicate optimal times based on your energy levels and past productivity patterns.

Example Use Case

You need 2 hours for focused work on a project:

  1. Click "Find Free Time"
  2. Set Duration: 2 hours
  3. Set Range: Next 3 workdays
  4. Set Hours: 9 AM - 5 PM
  5. Result: System suggests available 2-hour blocks within your parameters

Advanced Features

  • Energy Levels: Toggle "Consider Energy Levels" to find times when you're typically most productive
  • Buffer Time: Add padding before/after to avoid back-to-back commitments
  • Recurring Slots: Find regular time slots for recurring tasks

Troubleshooting

  • No Results? Try expanding your date range or reducing the duration
  • Unexpected Slots? Check your calendar sync status
  • Need More Options? Toggle "Show More Times" for additional suggestions
Pro Tip: Use the "Optimize for Focus Time" setting to find slots with minimal interruptions.
