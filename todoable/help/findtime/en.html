Find Time: Smart schedule with ease

What is Find Time?

Find Time helps you identify available time slots in your schedule for new tasks, family meetings, shared activities, or any other event that requires coordinating schedules.

Here's how it works:

  1. Select Participants

    Choose the family members or individuals who need to be available for the scheduled event.

  2. Choose Time Preference

    Select "Morning" or "Afternoon" to narrow down the search.

  3. View Availability
    • Highlighted slots: Indicate available times for all selected participants.
    • Empty slots: Indicate times when at least one participant is unavailable.
    • Grayed out slots: Indicate passed times.

  4. Select day and time

    Click on a highlighted time slot that works best for everyone.

  5. Create task

    Once you've selected a time, follow the instructions to easily add a new task to your to-do list for that specific time slot.

Tips

  • For best results, ensure all participants have their schedules accurately updated within the app. "Find Time" considers existing tasks and appointments when determining availability.
  • Scroll the dates to find time in the following weeks or months.
  • Adjust the time preference (morning/afternoon) or change the selected participants to refine your search.

Enjoy the convenience of "Find Time" and schedule your family events with ease!
